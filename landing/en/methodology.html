Our methodology

🚧 Translation in progress. For the full version, please read the French page — it's our source of truth.

Summary in 5 points

  1. Editorial framework: a fixed SOUL.md defines tone + absolute rules (never diagnose, always adapt to child's exact age, respect cultural context).
  2. Knowledge base of 131,000 characters: 8 thematic files + 6 peer-reviewed academic studies (Bowlby, Ainsworth, Baumrind, Piaget, Erikson, Nelsen, Rosenberg, Wertz et al.).
  3. AI engine (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini): generates response based strictly on this base.
  4. Systematic human review on all blog articles before publication.
  5. Authors: editorial personas representing specialties, transitioning to real licensed experts during 2026.

What ParentAtEase is NOT

Not a doctor, not a clinical psychologist, not an emergency service. When the bot detects sensitive subjects (suicidal ideation, suspected abuse, serious medical symptoms), it redirects to human professionals.
